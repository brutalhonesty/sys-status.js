When does this site send notifications to my subscribers?

For email & SMS, it only sends notifications to subscribers when you create or update an incident.

For webhooks, it sends notifications for components changing status, as well as incidents created or updated.

Do it send notifications when I change a component's status?

Only for webhook subscriptions.

What happens if you don't want to use notifications?

Allowing users to subscribe to updates of your status page is optional. Use the checkboxes in the settings tab to turn specific ones on and off.
